Help Keep the Archive Running

As the Train Simulator Mod Archive continues to grow in popularity, we've seen a wonderful increase in traffic and downloads from the community. However, this growth has also brought increased infrastructure costs that are becoming challenging to manage independently.

To accommodate the higher bandwidth and storage demands, we've had to upgrade our Cloudflare plan and expand our hosting capabilities. While we remain committed to keeping this archive accessible to everyone, the reality is that these operational costs are substantial.

Monthly Operating Costs

Cloudflare Workers (Paid Plan) $5.00
R2 Storage (~200GB) ~$3.00
Bandwidth & Operations ~$2.00
Total Monthly Cost ~$10.00

0%

Any contributions through Ko-fi go directly toward covering these monthly costs. Your support helps ensure that this valuable collection of Train Simulator Classic content remains preserved and accessible for years to come.

This project is entirely nonprofit and community-driven. While donations are never required, they make a meaningful difference in our ability to sustain and improve the archive.

Can't Donate? You Can Still Help!

Not everyone can contribute financially, and that's completely okay! Here are other ways you can support the archive:

  • Share the archive with fellow Train Simulator enthusiasts in forums, Discord servers, or social media
  • Join our Facebook group to stay updated and help spread awareness
  • Provide feedback on what content you'd like to see archived or features you'd like added
  • Simply using the archive and keeping this content alive in the community helps preserve Train Sim history

Thank you for being part of this preservation effort!
